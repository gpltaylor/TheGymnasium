Why Join Us?

We have firm beliefs in the RIGHT way to train. For too long large gym chains have given you a card to swipe, equipment to use and no support throughout. The Gymnasium Chorley breaks that mould. No distractions, just pure fitness and quality exercise.

The Gymnasium is bringing you that fitness level you've always longed for in a fun and friendly atmosphere. Keeping training functional making everyday movements easier and shaping the body to how we've always wanted.

The Gymnasium Chorley offers an open gym with a difference. How many times have you walked in the gym and not had a clue what to do? We provide constant support and advice plus daily workout routines for you to work through on our chalk board. This way we ensure your quality of exercise keeps your body guessing.

To add to that we have our weekly indoor class timetable. Providing you with quality instructor led group exercise with a difference. Attendance at these classes will only get you addicted to exercise with our WORK HARD, PLAY HARD, HAVE FUN mentality.

The ultimate fitness experience is here. Let your stress out on a punch bag or hammer away at our tractor tyre!

If you want to get fit you'll find a way. If you don't, you'll find an excuse!!
